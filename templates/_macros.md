## III. Risk Level

| Level | Meaning | Typical Characteristics | Recommended Response |
|------|---------|-------------------------|----------------------|
| High | High probability or high impact | May directly cause objective failure, major rework, or external breach | Escalate immediately, assign dedicated owner, prepare mitigation and rollback plan |
| Medium | Medium probability or impact | May cause scope/schedule fluctuation but tolerable | Track in regular meetings, prepare mitigation measures |
| Low | Low probability and low impact | Minor disturbance, easily absorbed | Log and observe, handle as needed |
